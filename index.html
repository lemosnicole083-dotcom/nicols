About me

I am an award-winning actress and international model known for bringing emotion, elegance, and authenticity into every project I take on. After studying dramatic arts at some of the world’s most prestigious performing arts institutions like Juilliard School, I began building a career defined by versatility and powerful storytelling. Over the years, I have starred in internationally acclaimed productions, including series such as The Vampire Diaries and Stranger Things, as well as major feature films ranging from psychological thrillers like Sinners to blockbuster productions within the Marvel Studios universe. My performances have earned recognition from some of the entertainment industry’s most prestigious awards, including the Oscar and Golden Globe.

Alongside my acting career, I became a recognized presence in the fashion industry, collaborating with luxury brands, appearing on international magazine covers, and attending some of the world’s most iconic events and red carpets. My work as a model reflects the same confidence, versatility, and artistic vision that define my performances on screen.

Beyond acting and fashion, I have also explored music as a creative passion, releasing songs that connected with audiences worldwide. Through every project, I continue to build a career driven by storytelling, artistry, and timeless expression.
